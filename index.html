About Gary, Indiana

Gary, Indiana, is a vibrant city located along the southern shore of Lake Michigan, known for its rich industrial history and diverse culture. Once a thriving hub for steel production, Gary has seen significant changes in its economy and demographics over the years. Today, the city is home to approximately 75,000 residents and is actively working towards revitalization and community improvement. Notable landmarks include the historic Gary Aquatorium, the Art Deco City Hall, and the scenic Marquette Park, which offers stunning views of the lake. Gary's ongoing economic development initiatives aim to attract new businesses and foster growth in various sectors.
